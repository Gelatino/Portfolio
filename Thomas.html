Thomas

Berlin 2018

Anna has left Thomas heartbroken. After a tough break up he now attempts to contact her to recover a set of matrioshka dolls from her but she won’t answer.

Film entirely with friends and no budget, Thomas was a long and ambitious experiment with way too many locations, non-actors, and no professional crew, in which I wanted to look into the feelings of a young man with a broken heart.
