Potato Potato

Berlin - 2020

Potato Potato

Writer and Director

David, the chef of the restaurant, has invested years on Potato Potato. Hannah has been working with him for a year. Today, Kam, the owner of the restaurant, comes in to let them know he has sold the restaurant. David has to either buy the restaurant back or accept that his beloved restaurant is over.

With Potato Potato I try to explore how different people deal with things coming to an end. In this case, the end of a restaurant that means everything to Peter and Hannah.
