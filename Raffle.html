The Cause

I want to create a short film about Emili, an aspiring actress from Mexico, facing challenges in her pursuit of fame. Emili and her mother run a small restaurant in a forgotten town. When a big company announces a casting call, Emili sees it as her big break. Supported by her friends, she struggles with self-doubt and her mother's pressure to stay and help.

The Reason

I want to tell the story of those with big dreams but difficult circumstances. "Oneira" isn't just about talent or hard work; it's about trying against the odds. Many people don't reach their dreams, and I want to celebrate their stories and show they matter.
